A New Way of Travelling

From a female & queer perspective, open to everyone. Meet inspiring people around the world to work together and play together. Connect to create businesses, art, projects and technology. Or just travel for fun.

Isn't it crazy that the decision whether we trust a person or not is still mainly based on reviews? If a user has not many reviews or none at all, we are not likely to trust her or him. Bad reviews are hardly found, since writing those are socially uncomfortable. Isn't there a better way of getting a clearer idea of who is sitting behind the screen? A smarter way of asking for reviews? We are creating a matching algorithm based on information we collect on a joined approach, using Machine Learning.
